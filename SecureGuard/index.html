How Impossible Travel Detection Works
When the same user logs in from two different locations within a short time window, we calculate the minimum required travel speed between those coordinates.
🔴 IMPOSSIBLE — Speed > 900 km/h (faster than commercial aircraft)  |  🟠 SUSPICIOUS — Speed > 200 km/h  |  ✅ NORMAL — Plausible travel time
